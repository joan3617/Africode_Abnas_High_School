About Us.

Abnas High School is a mixed day and boarding school located in Bomet County, Kenya. It is a Christian school that offers the 8-4-4 curriculum. The school is known for its high academic standards and excellent performance in national examinations. It has a dedicated team of teachers who are committed to providing quality education to the students. The school also offers a wide range of extracurricular activities to help students develop their talents and skills. Abnas High School is the best high school in Bomet County and is a great place for students to learn and grow.
